Premium Cat Litter for Your Feline Friend

Our Experties :

Outstanding Odor Control:

Enjoy a fresh-smelling home as our litter traps and locks in odors, ensuring your living space remains pleasant and odor-free.

Quick Clumping & Easy Scooping:

Our naturally-absorbent corn litter forms firm clumps, making waste removal a breeze without the need for strenuous scraping or chiseling.

99% Dust Free:

Say goodbye to dusty litter. Our corn-based formula is free from silica dust, providing a clean and safe environment for both you and your cat.

Pet, People & Planet Friendly:

Made from sustainable corn and free from added chemicals, our litter is a responsible choice that promotes the well-being of your pet, your family, and the environment.

Lightweight:

Experience the convenience of our lightweight litter. It delivers exceptional performance without the heavy weight, giving your back a much-needed break.

Flushable* & Septic Safe:

Tested and proven to be flushable* and safe for septic and sewer systems, our litter saves you the hassle of frequent trips to the trash bin.
